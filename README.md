# CYBER-CRISIS-SIMULATION

Join us to investigate a potential breach and collaborate as incident responders, executives, legal, and PR teams to protect millions of customers. Step into a real-world cyber crisis with DaemonTech Commercial Bank! 🔒 Plus, it's great practice for our upcoming CTF. Can you uncover what happened before it's too late? 🏆

In this simulation, you will work in teams alongside each other to piece together the details of a potential breach, and safeguard millions of customers' data.
